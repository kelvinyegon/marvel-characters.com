Marvel Characters

spider man

Spider-Man is a beloved superhero from Marvel Comics, created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. Introduced in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962, Spider-Man quickly became an icon due to his relatable story and unique powers. Known for his ability to swing through cityscapes on webs, climb walls, and sense danger with his "spidey-sense," Spider-Man is Peter Parker, an ordinary young man who gains extraordinary abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider

Captain America

Captain America is another iconic superhero from Marvel Comics, created by writer Joe Simon and artist Jack Kirby. He first appeared in Captain America Comics #1 in 1941, during World War II, and was designed to be a patriotic symbol fighting against the Axis powers. The character’s real name is Steve Rogers, a frail young man from Brooklyn who, after being injected with the Super Soldier Serum, transforms into the peak of human strength, agility, and endurance, becoming Captain America

antman

Ant-Man is a superhero in the Marvel Comics universe, known for his ability to shrink in size while increasing in strength, as well as his capacity to communicate with and control ants. The character has been portrayed by multiple individuals across different storylines, but the most prominent versions are Hank Pym and Scott Lang
